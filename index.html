Research Interests. I develop graph machine learning (GML) techniques, with a focus on but not limited to graph neural networks and graph foundation models.

  1. Accelerate Electronic design automation (EDA) tasks by leveraging GML techniques.
  2. Develop open-source GML tools to support applications in EDA.
Research Keywords: Graph Neural Networks, AI for EDA.
