Research Interests. I develop graph machine learning (GML) techniques and its applications on electronic design automation (EDA) tasks, with a focus on but not limited to graph neural networks (GNN4EDA), graph foundation models (GFM4EDA), neuro-symbolic GML4EDA and trustworthy GML4EDA.

  1. Develop neuro-symbolic and trustworthy AI-native EDA techinques via GML.
  2. Develop open-source GML tools to support applications in EDA.
  3. Develop large circuit models (LCMs).
Research Keywords: Graph machine learning, AI for EDA.
